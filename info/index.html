Matt Zolly is an audio-visual maverick, hailing from San Francisco, CA. He’s inspired by sites and sounds, especially when the two come together
harmoniously. Since moving to Los Angeles a decade ago, he’s worn a number of hats to cover his bald head; including, but not limited to
Director, Creative Director, Executive Producer, and Music Supervisor. He’s held positions at William Morris Endeavor, Atlantic Records,
Indie-Pop Management, and Prettybird. In 2013 he founded the iconic commercial and music video production company, Snow Beach.
